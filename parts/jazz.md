Jazz
===

Jazz has several stages of evolution: New Orlean, swing, bebop, cool jazz, post-bop, free jazz, regional varieties and fusions.

Main topics on jazz are:
- [**harmony**](jazz_harmony.md), including [**lego**](lego.md)
- [**solo**](jazz_solo.md)
- rhythm
